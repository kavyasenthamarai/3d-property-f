How It Works

We provide an immersive 3D experience that allows you to explore properties from every angle. Navigate, zoom, and get detailed views all from your device.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to explore properties in 3D and interact with the platform:

  • Step 1: Select Your PropertyChoose a property from our list. Each listing has high-quality 3D models, 360-degree views, and detailed floor plans.
  • Step 2: Explore in 3DOnce you’ve selected a property, you can begin exploring it in 3D. Use controls to zoom, rotate, and move through rooms and spaces.
  • Step 3: Interact with FeaturesClick on objects within the 3D model to see detailed information about features, including furniture, rooms, and appliances.
  • Step 4: Customize Your ViewUse the customization tools to visualize potential changes, such as new paint colors or furniture arrangements (coming soon!).

Interactive Features

Our platform offers exciting features for a richer exploration:

  • 360-Degree Views: Explore properties with full control of the viewing angle and direction.
  • Clickable Elements: Click on different parts of the property to learn more about specific features like rooms, furniture, and appliances.
  • Real-Time Lighting Adjustments: Change the time of day to see how the property looks in different lighting conditions, from sunrise to sunset.

Learn About 3D Viewing

3D viewing allows you to:

  • Experience Immersive Exploration: Navigate through rooms and spaces as if you were there in person, providing a more realistic feel than traditional photos.
  • Gain a Better Understanding: Visualize the layout and dimensions of a property in 3D, ensuring you're making the best decision.
  • Interactive and Fun: Engage with the content dynamically with features like zooming, rotating, and walkthroughs.
